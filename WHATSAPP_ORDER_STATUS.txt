================================================================================
                          CORRECTED PHONE FLOW
================================================================================

PRIORITY 1: Check if vendor has assigned delivery boy
└─> YES: Send WhatsApp to DELIVERY BOY PHONE
└─> NO: Go to PRIORITY 2

PRIORITY 2: No delivery boy assigned
└─> Send WhatsApp to CENTRAL ROCKETWHEEL NUMBER
└─> Central team assigns delivery boy

❌ NEVER: Send to VENDOR PHONE
(vendor.phone is REMOVED from chain)

Result: WhatsApp message goes ONLY to RocketWheel Delivery System
